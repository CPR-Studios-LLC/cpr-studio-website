Shoptics Setup

Connect your Shopify store to Shoptics with a custom app access token — a secure, revocable key you create inside your own Shopify admin. About 2 minutes, one time.

Why a custom app token instead of "Sign in with Shopify"?

Shopify lets every store create its own private custom app — a built-in feature designed exactly for connecting trusted tools. You create the app inside your own admin, choose precisely which permissions it gets, and Shopify issues a token only you can see. You stay in control: uninstall the custom app at any time and Shoptics' access dies instantly. Your Shopify password is never involved.

How we protect your store

  • Your token is encrypted (AES-256) at rest on our servers and never appears on your glasses, in logs, or in the app again after you paste it.
  • Nothing changes in your store without your tap. The voice agent can only propose actions — every draft order is shown to you in full on your glasses, and only your confirmation tap executes it.
  • We don't keep your sales data. Orders are read on demand to answer your question, then discarded — nothing is warehoused beyond a 60-second dashboard cache.
  • Voice audio is never stored. It streams from your glasses to our transcription provider and exists only for the seconds it takes to transcribe.
  • Revoke anytime. Uninstall the custom app in Shopify, or tap Disconnect in Shoptics — either kills access immediately.

Create your access token

Open your Shopify settings

In your Shopify admin, go to Settings → Apps and sales channels.

Enable custom app development

Click Develop apps, then Allow custom app development (Shopify asks you to confirm twice — this just unlocks the feature for your store).

Create the app

Click Create an app and name it Shoptics. The app developer is you.

Grant the four permissions

Open Configure Admin API scopes and check exactly these four:

read_orders read_products read_customers write_draft_orders

Then click Save. These let Shoptics read your sales numbers and create the draft orders you confirm — nothing more. It cannot touch payouts, settings, themes, or published orders.

Install the app and reveal your token

Go to the API credentials tab, click Install app, then Reveal token once. Copy the token — it starts with shpat_. Shopify only shows it one time.

Paste it into Shoptics

Open Shoptics in the Even Realities app on your phone. Enter your store domain (like your-store.myshopify.com) and paste the token, then tap Connect. Shoptics verifies everything instantly.

Put on your glasses

Launch Shoptics from your G2. Your live dashboard appears — tap once to ask your store anything.

Heads up: the token is shown by Shopify only once. If you lose it, no problem — uninstall the custom app and reinstall it to generate a fresh one, then reconnect in Shoptics.

Troubleshooting

"Your custom app is missing API scopes"

One of the four permissions wasn't checked before installing. Go back to your custom app → Configuration → edit Admin API scopes, check the missing one (Shoptics tells you which), save, then open API credentials and uninstall/reinstall the app to mint a new token. Paste the new token into Shoptics.

"Shopify rejected that token"

Usually a copy/paste miss — the token must start with shpat_ and be copied in full. Reveal-once means a previously viewed token can't be re-copied; reinstall the custom app for a fresh one.

"Enter your .myshopify.com store domain"

Use your store's internal domain (find it under Settings → Domains), not your custom storefront domain. It looks like your-store.myshopify.com.
