📧 GlassMail

Read and reply to emails from your smart glasses

AI-powered email management for Even Realities G2 glasses

Stay Connected, Hands-Free

GlassMail brings your Gmail inbox to your Even Realities G2 smart glasses. Read emails at a glance, generate AI-powered replies, and send responses — all without reaching for your phone.

📬 Inbox at a Glance

See your latest emails displayed right in your field of view. Swipe to navigate, tap to read.

🤖 AI-Powered Replies

Generate smart, contextual responses with a single tap. Customize with your own rules and preferences.

🎤 Voice Context

Add specific details by speaking. The AI incorporates your voice input into the perfect reply.

✉️ Tap to Send

Review the AI-generated draft, then send instantly. Stay responsive without breaking your flow.

Your Privacy Matters

GlassMail connects to your Gmail account to provide email functionality. Here's what we access and why:

  • Read emails: To display your inbox and email content on your glasses
  • Send emails: To send replies you compose and approve
  • Email metadata: Sender names, subjects, and dates for display

We never store your emails on our servers. All email data stays between you and Google.
