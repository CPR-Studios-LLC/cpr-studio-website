Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Introduction

GlassMail ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our email application for Even Realities G2 smart glasses.

Information We Collect

  • Google Account: When you sign in, we receive your email address and access to your Gmail inbox via Google OAuth.
  • Email Content: We read your emails to display them and generate AI replies. Email content is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.
  • Voice Input: If you use voice features, audio is sent to Deepgram for transcription. We do not store audio recordings.
  • Usage Data: We track the number of AI drafts generated for billing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your emails on your G2 glasses
  • Generate AI-powered reply drafts using Google Gemini
  • Transcribe voice input for contextual replies
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions

Third-Party Services

  • Google: Gmail API for email access, Gemini for AI replies
  • Deepgram: Speech-to-text transcription
  • Stripe: Payment processing

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. OAuth tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time through your Google account settings.

Your Rights

You can disconnect GlassMail from your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions. This immediately revokes our access to your emails.
